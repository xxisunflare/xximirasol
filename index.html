Past and Present

I worked in bank as an Assistant Manager, where I applied my interpersonal and multi-tasking skills as I managed branch operations. I realized that there are low-efficient tasks that can be improved through Data Science (innovative technology). To start, I’ve taken a Data Science Bootcamp and I’m currently self-studying. I hope to enhance my knowledge in the field to have a job and interact with like-minded individuals in the industry. I will use my learnings to improve my portfolio and help businesses make data-driven decisions. I also hope to apply them in my own business someday.

Future

Due to COVID-19, banks were affected as people replace branch visits with digital services, and that’s how I discovered my interest in innovative technology through Data Science. I wanted to use my skills to improve how banks or other businesses operate for the benefit of the company, its clients, and employees. My goal is to upskill to have a job in the tech industry and make a positive impact.
